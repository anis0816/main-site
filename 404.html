About

Hi, and welcome to my website. My name is Anis, I was born in Bosnia on the 16th of August 1988. At the age of six, I moved to the beautiful city of Des Moines, Iowa, where I had spent most of my childhood life.

After finishing High School in Des Moines, I decided to try something completely new and moved to Austria. My family and I eventually settled in the charming city of Wasserburg am Inn near the Bavarian alps.

I can speak English, German, and Bosnian fluently. I have way too many Hobbies! One of my favorite hobbies is to go hiking in the Alps. But I also love to visit the nearby forests or lakes! And when I do, I take lots of photographs of nature. Another hobby that I have is woodworking, I just love the scent of wood.

More

Ever since I was a child I absolutely loved working with technology, whether it was recording a video on VHS, exploring the operating system, or just playing Super Mario on Nintendo.

That's exactly why I decided I would follow my lifelong dream and make it true. In 2019, I made a life-changing decision by attending college and getting my education in computer science with the focus on IT specialist for system integration.

And so far it has been a dream come true.
